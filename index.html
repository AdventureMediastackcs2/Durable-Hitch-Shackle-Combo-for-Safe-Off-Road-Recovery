Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: What is a hitch shackle used for?
 A hitch shackle connects recovery straps, ropes, or chains to a vehicle’s hitch receiver for safe towing or recovery operations.

Q2: Can this combo fit any vehicle hitch?
 The Mohawk 1.25 Receiver fits standard 1.25-inch hitches. For larger 2-inch receivers, adapters are available separately.

Q3: Is this combo safe for synthetic ropes?
 Yes, the Jowl Split Shackle features smooth, rounded edges to protect synthetic ropes and soft shackles from abrasion.

Q4: How strong is the Jowl 5/8 Split Shackle?
 The forged construction supports heavy loads suitable for off-road recovery and professional towing applications.

Q5: How do I maintain the shackle combo?
 Keep it clean, lubricate the pin occasionally, and inspect for wear before every use to ensure consistent safety.

Q6: Is the blue finish durable?
 Absolutely. The powder-coated blue surface is designed to resist rust, corrosion, and fading even in harsh outdoor conditions.
